About Therese

STILL

Therese Marzouk  ·  Speaker  ·  Workforce Strategist

Therese Marzouk has spent fifteen years in workforce development watching people freeze. Not because they're weak — but because nobody told them what to do with the weight while they were still carrying it.

She knows what it feels like to have a project due on Friday and a hole in your chest. To stand in a parking lot with a brand new dog and a grief you haven't had time to process yet. To keep feeding your kids, answering emails, showing up — not because you're okay, but because you don't have the option to not be okay.

"I can't do this for you. But I will be here with you while you walk through it."

What she learned — the hard way, across years of personal loss and professional reinvention — is that moving through the hard thing IS the processing. You don't have to feel ready. You just have to pick one thing and start there.

Science nerd. Word nerd. Former owner of Word Knives. She brings precise, specific, alive language into every room — and a "Girls Will Be Girls" tshirt into every hotel room.
