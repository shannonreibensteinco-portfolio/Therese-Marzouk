How Therese thinks
01

You don't have to feel ready.

The avocado doesn't warn you. Day three: not yet. Day 3.5: too late. The window is real. The move is now.

02

There is no right way to feel about it.

Grief doesn't resolve in the four weeks before your project is due. Your kids still need to be fed. Both things are true at the same time.

03

Moving is the processing.

One small action taken forward — even sideways, even imperfectly — is how you find out what you're made of.
